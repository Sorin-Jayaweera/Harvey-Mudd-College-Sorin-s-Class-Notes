$$
\begin{align}
 400mv = \left( 1+\frac{rf}{rn} \right) \left( \frac{Rg}{Rp+Rg} \right)Vp_{1} \\
Rf = 2000 \Omega\\
Vp_{1} = 5v \\
Rn = 1000 \Omega \\
\implies \\
400mV = (1+2)\left( \frac{Rg}{Rp+Rg} \right)5 \\
\frac{0.4}{15} = \frac{Rg}{Rp+Rg} \\
0.026(Rp + Rg) = Rg \\
0.026 Rp = Rg(1-0.026)
\end{align}
$$

$$
\begin{align}
0.026Rp = 0.97 Rg \\
Rp = 37.3 Rg
\end{align}
$$
