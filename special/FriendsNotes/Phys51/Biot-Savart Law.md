##  Biot-Savart Law
 > [!abstract]+ Law
 > 
Magnetic equivalent of [[Coulomb's Law]]. $$
d\vec{B} =  \frac{\mu_{0}}{4\pi}  \frac{ id\vec{l}\times \hat{r}}{r^{2} },
$$ where $\mu_{0} = 4\pi\times 10^{-7} \;\text{ T}\cdot \text{m}\cdot \mathrm{A}^{-1}$ is the permeability of free space
